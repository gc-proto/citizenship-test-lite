Canadian citizenship test study guide

From Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada

This is the only official study guide for the Canadian citizenship test. The guide is always available at no cost and is your main resource to study for the citizenship test. All the information you need for the test is in the study guide. If you use any other material to prepare for the citizenship test, you do so at your own risk.

There are very small differences between the print, audio and electronic versions of the study guide. These differences won't affect your ability to prepare for the citizenship test.

Other study tools

Practice test
